FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need trading experience to join?
No. We have courses and mentorship plans designed for complete beginners. The Starter plan and Foundations course are perfect starting points.
What broker do you recommend?
We primarily use and recommend Exness for its low spreads on XAUUSD and fast execution — ideal for all our EAs and strategies. XM is also supported.
Are the EAs for MT4 or MT5?
All current FAFX EAs are built for MetaTrader 5 (MT5). If you need an MT4 version, contact us — some can be ported on request.
How do I receive the EA after purchase?
After payment is confirmed, we'll send the .ex5 file and full installation instructions to your email and WhatsApp within 24 hours.
Is mentorship conducted live or recorded?
Pro and Elite plans include weekly live sessions via Zoom/Telegram. All sessions are recorded and available for replay within 48 hours.
Can I cancel my mentorship plan?
Yes. Mentorship plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with no penalty. Just send us a message before your next billing date.
